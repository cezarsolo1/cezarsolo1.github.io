## What it is

MechTire is a robotic system for automating tire changes at tire shops. Unlike existing solutions that use large fixed industrial arms, we use mobile robots — reducing space requirements and making installation viable for real garages.

## My role

Co-founder. Working on business strategy, customer development, and technical direction.

## Status

Early stage — research, prototyping, and customer discovery ongoing at TU/e.
